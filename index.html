The purpose of the Lowe's Customer Satisfaction Survey conducted by Lowe's is to collect feedback from customers and improve services. The survey can be done on Lowe's official website at lowes.com or at lowes.com/survey. Following are some of the main requirements for the survey



Lowe's Survey Requirements


  1. Survey Entry Receipt: You need a receipt received after making a purchase from a Lowe's store, which has a 12-digit survey code on it. This code allows you to enter the survey.
  2. Age: You must be at least 18 years of age to participate in the survey.
  3. Citizenship: Participants must be residents of the US, the District of Columbia, or Canada. Residents of Quebec are not included.
  4. Internet Access: Since the survey is conducted online, you must have an internet connection and a computer or smartphone.
  5. Time Limit: The survey code provided on the Lowe's receipt can only be used within 10 days from the date it was taken.
  6. Language: The survey is available in English or Spanish. You must understand either language.
  7. Participation Limit: An individual may take the survey only once per month.
  8. Prizes: Completing a survey usually entitles you to a sweepstake where you can win gift cards provided by Lowe's. The conditions for qualifying for a prize may be as per the sweepstakes rules.

How to take the Lowes Survey using lowes.com/survey?


  1. Go to the lowes.com/survey link on your Lowe's receipt.
  2. Enter the 12-digit survey code on your receipt.
  3. Answer the survey questions and provide feedback about your experience.
  4. Finally, you can provide contact information to participate in the sweepstakes.
  5. This survey is designed to improve the customer experience and help the company improve its services and products.
